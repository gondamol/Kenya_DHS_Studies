# 07_grade — Certainty of evidence (GRADE)

- **`grade_guidance.md`** — the five GRADE domains, certainty levels, and this review's
  specific indirectness triggers.
- **`grade_sof_template.csv`** — one row per outcome to record domain judgements and the
  plain-language summary, mirroring a GRADEpro Summary-of-Findings table.

Two assessors, consensus. Final SoF tables are produced in GRADEpro GDT and exported to
the manuscript.
