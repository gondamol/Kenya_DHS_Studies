# RACI matrix — who does what

**R** = Responsible (does the work) · **A** = Accountable (owns sign-off; one per task)
· **C** = Consulted · **I** = Informed.

Roles: **NWA** (lead/guarantor), **JO** (2nd reviewer/supervision), **LH** (clinical),
**NAO** (data/analysis), **Lib** (medical librarian, TBC), **Sup** (supervisor(s), TBC).

| Review task | NWA | JO | LH | NAO | Lib | Sup |
|-------------|-----|----|----|-----|-----|-----|
| Finalise master search strategy | A/R | C | I | C | R | I |
| PRESS peer review of search | A | I | — | I | R | — |
| Run database searches | A | C | — | R | C | — |
| Deduplicate (Rayyan) | A/R | C | — | C | — | — |
| Title/abstract screening | A/R | R | — | C | — | I |
| Full-text screening | A/R | R | C | C | — | I |
| Screening adjudication (3rd reviewer) | I | I | — | — | — | A/R |
| Data extraction (duplicate) | A/R | R | — | R | — | I |
| Author contact for missing data | A/R | C | — | C | — | I |
| Risk of bias (RoB 2, duplicate) | A/R | R | — | C | — | C |
| Meta-analysis (R pipeline) | A/R | C | — | R | — | C |
| GRADE assessment (duplicate) | A/R | R | — | C | — | C |
| Patient-characteristics analysis | A/R | C | C | R | — | C |
| Draft manuscript | A/R | C | C | C | — | C |
| Critical revision / approval | A | R | R | R | I | R |
| Submission & correspondence | A/R | I | I | I | — | I |

> Two-reviewer tasks (screening, extraction, RoB, GRADE) are done **independently** then
> reconciled. The 3rd-reviewer/adjudication role should sit with someone *not* doing the
> primary screening on that item — pencilled in for a supervisor; confirm at onboarding.
