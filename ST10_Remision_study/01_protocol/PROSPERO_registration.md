## Key registered parameters (cross-check against the protocol)
- **Design:** systematic review + meta-analysis of RCTs.
- **Primary outcome:** T2DM remission (HbA1c <48 mmol/mol ≥3 months off all
  glucose-lowering pharmacotherapy; 2021 consensus).
- **Secondary (exploratory):** drug-free BP normalisation (<130/80 mmHg ≥6 months off
  antihypertensives).
- **Setting:** World Bank low-/middle-income economies.
- **Final search:** within 30 days of registration (target ~28 June 2026).
